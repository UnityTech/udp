Managing IAP on UDP portal#



You can manage IAP on UDP portal.

  1. Login in UDP portal and select your game.

  2. Go to IAP by choosing GAME Information > In-App Purchases. If you have created IAP items in the Unity Editor, you can see these items.

  1. You can add, edit, or delete IAP items.
